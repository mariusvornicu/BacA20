F.A.Q.

1. What is the purpose of this app?

The MuseumGO app was designed to enhance the interaction between the museums' visitors and the items that are exhibited in the museum.

2. What museums do we present?

Our app was designed for the mostly visited museums from Romania.

3. Is this app legally created?

This app was created in partnership with the museums that we present.
Each of the museums provided us photos and descrpitons of their most valuable exhibits with the purpose of enhancing the visitors' experience.

4. How we are providing the museum experience?

We are using APIs to provide the support for QR Codes, the Bot Assistant as well as the Virtual Tour for the gallery.

5. Want to know more about us?

If there are any other questions or curiosities about our work, please contact us using the Contact Us form from the Home page!
